Capitol Words

Looking through various API directories I found one from the Sunlight Foundation called 'Capitol Words' that processes the text of the Congressional Record, allowing you to look at word frequencies: who said what, how often, when, etc.

I'm looking into the documentation, I think I can have an example piped into this blog pretty soon.
